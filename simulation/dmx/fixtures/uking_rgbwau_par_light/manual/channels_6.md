# 6 Channels Mode (d001)
# Confirmed by Sina against the manual.

| Channel | Value   | Function                                       |
|---------|---------|------------------------------------------------|
| CH1     | 000-255 | R dimming — Red dimming, from dark to bright   |
| CH2     | 000-255 | G dimming — Green dimming, from dark to bright |
| CH3     | 000-255 | B dimming — Blue dimming, from dark to bright  |
| CH4     | 000-255 | W dimming — White dimming, from dark to bright |
| CH5     | 000-255 | Y dimming — Amber tone light, from dark to bright |
| CH6     | 000-255 | P dimming — Purple dimming, from dark to bright |
